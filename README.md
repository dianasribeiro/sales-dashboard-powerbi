# 📊 Sales Performance Dashboard | Power BI

An interactive **Power BI dashboard** developed to analyze sales performance through financial and commercial KPIs. The dashboard provides clear insights into revenue, costs, profitability, and sales performance across products, supervisors, and sales representatives.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

The goal of this dashboard is to support data-driven decision-making by transforming raw sales data into meaningful and interactive visualizations.

Users can explore business performance using dynamic filters and gain insights into key financial metrics and sales trends.

---

## 📈 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- 💰 Total Revenue
- 💸 Total Cost
- 📊 Profit Margin

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

The dashboard includes:

- Revenue by Product Group
- Profit Margin by Product Group
- Profit Margin by Product Line
- Revenue Trend Over Time
- Profit Margin by Supervisor
- Profit Margin by Sales Representative
- Detailed Revenue and Cost Table by Supervisor and Sales Representative
- KPI Cards for quick performance monitoring
